What Drives Me

My Vision

Where I want to go, what I believe in, and the kind of developer I'm becoming.

"I believe great software is built by people who care — about their craft, about their users, and about the impact their work has on the world. My vision as a developer is to keep that care at the centre of everything I build."

In the next few years, I want to deepen my expertise in backend architecture and cloud technologies, contribute to open-source projects, and ultimately lead engineering efforts on products that solve real challenges for Nepali businesses and communities.

Grow into Full-Stack

Expand beyond backend C# into cloud-native development, microservices, and modern frontend frameworks like React or Blazor. Become a developer who can build an entire product end-to-end.

Build for Nepal's Ecosystem

Contribute to local startups and tech products that create meaningful impact in Nepal. I want my work to matter to the community I come from.

Embrace Cloud & DevOps

Learn Azure and AWS to deploy and scale applications in the cloud. Understand CI/CD pipelines, Docker, and infrastructure as code.

Open Source Contributions

Give back to the developer community by contributing to open-source projects and sharing reusable tools and libraries that help others.

Never Stop Learning

Stay curious about emerging technologies — AI/ML integration, distributed systems, and performance engineering. Curiosity is my greatest professional asset.

Mentor & Lead

Grow into a senior role where I can mentor junior developers, lead technical decisions, and help build engineering cultures where quality and collaboration thrive.

Want to Build Something Together?

I'm always open to interesting projects, collaborations, and new opportunities.
